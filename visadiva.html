Questions

Before you ask

Which countries do you cover?

Our work spans major destinations across Africa, the Americas, Europe and beyond. Every engagement begins with a consultation to confirm we’re the right fit for your route.

Can you really speed up a visa?

Where expedited channels and relationships exist, yes — timing depends on the country and category. What we always accelerate is preparation: complete, precise applications avoid the delays that catch most applicants.

My visa was refused. Can you help?

Refusals are often recoverable. We review the decision, identify what went wrong, and design a stronger reapplication or appeal.

Do you work with organisations as well as individuals?

Yes — cultural institutions, festivals, production companies and businesses moving teams and talent across borders are a core part of our practice.
